# PUSH (chest, triceps, and latissimus dorsi)
- Bench Press (4 sets of 6-8) ← chest
- Incline Bench Press (4 sets of 6-8) ← chest
- Shoulder Press (4 sets of 6-8) ← shoulders
- Rope Pulldown + Rope Pullovers (4 sets of 6-8) ← triceps
- Lateral Raises (3 sets of 12-15) ← lats
- Cardio Incline Treadmill/Stair Master (30min) ← 10.0% Incline + 3.0 Speed

# PULL (back and biceps)
- Lat Pulldowns (4 sets of 6-8) ← upper back
- Seated Rows/Chest Supported Rows (4 sets of 6-8) ← lower back
- Preacher Curls (4 sets of 6-8) ← biceps
- Pull Ups (4 sets of 6-8) ← upper/lower back
- Hammer Curls (3 sets of 12-15 each arm) ← biceps
- Cardio Incline Treadmill/Stair Master (30min) ← 10.0% Incline + 3.0 Speed

# Legs (quadriceps femoris, hamstrings, and calves)
- Leg Press (4 sets of 6-8) ← quads
- Hamstring Curls (4 sets of 6-8) ← hamstrings
- Leg Extensions (4 sets of 6-8) ← quads
- Calf Raises (4 sets of 6-8) ← calves

# NOTES
- the slower the better ← means more control
- volume over reps within range → **IF more than 8 increase the weight**
- solo/duo should roughly take an hour or a little over
